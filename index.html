Return On Investment (ROI)

Research shows that employers can expect a $3-4 return for $1 invested in back-up care due to less absenteeism, better work product and a reducation in employee turnover.

Why OndeCare?

OndeCare's Concierge Family Care Program is an employee benefit that can contribute to employee attraction and retention stratagies, address the cost of productivity loss due to unplanned absences (estimated as 6.7% of payroll), and prepare deadline driven businesses for the key challenges of tomorrow's workfore:

  • The momentum of the work-life movement
  • The increase of Millenialls in the workforce
  • The aging Baby Boomer Population

Work Life integration has been underway for over a decade but today more than 50% of working mothers and fathers say juggling work and family life is difficult. Both consider leaving the workplace beacause of it. Suprisingly, men are increasingly more likely to change jobs (67%) than women (57%).

By 2025, 75% of the global workforce will be made up of Generation-Y (Millenials), of whom 62% say on-site or subsidised childcare is important as opposed to 47% of the Boomer Generations

Nearly half of adults in their 40s and 50s have a parent older than 65 and have children of their own or are supporting a grown child. Finally, 15% of middle-aged adults are providing financial support to both an aging parent and a child. This number will only continue to rise
